My opinion on the matter

I personally think that no AI will ever replace a teacher.
They are genuinely fun people to be around with. It can be
a lot of fun, especially in class, when the teacher brings
their own unique energy, humor, and perspective to the lesson.
Teachers not only share knowledge but also create an environment
where students feel supported, motivated, and encouraged to think
critically. Their ability to adapt to each student’s needs and provide
personalized guidance is something that AI simply can’t replicate.
